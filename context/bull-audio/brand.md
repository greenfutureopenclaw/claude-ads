# Bull Audio — Brand & Product Guide

## Brand Overview
- **Name:** Bull Audio
- **Website:** bullaudio.ph (Shopify)
- **Stores:** Shopee, Lazada, TikTok Shop
- **Parent/Distributor:** FlowerStore
- **Launch:** October 2025
- **Market:** Philippines (Gen Z + Millennials)
- **Claimed roots:** Ibiza, Spain
- **Tagline:** "Power You Can Feel, Style You Can Flex"

## Brand Identity
- **Palette:** Black (primary), White (typography), Orange (accent on Alpha only) — NO yellow
- **Logo:** Bold uppercase horizontal layout, full-color + white-on-dark variant
- **Aesthetic:** Dark, modern, nightclub/Ibiza-inspired
- **Visual language:** RGB/neon lighting from products provides color contrast

## Brand Pillars
1. Studio-Grade Sound
2. Built to Move and Last
3. Designed to Stand Out
4. Luxury Made Affordable

## Brand Voice
- Energetic, youthful, confident
- Mixes English + Filipino slang ("sulit," "birit," "e-numan")
- Challenges premium brands directly
- Anti-logo-tax messaging

## Key Copy
- **Hero:** "Power Meets Design, at a Price You'll Love 🤘"
- **Mission:** "Our goal is to make premium sound accessible. We built this brand for those who live loud, stay creative, and believe good sound should be both powerful and affordable."
- **Self-description:** "The audio trendsetter with roots in Ibiza" / "a 100% Filipino-based and focused brand"
- **Brand challenge copy:** "When you pay a premium price, how much of that is for the sound and how much is for the logo?"

## Lineup Segmentation

### Personal Tier (5–20W RMS)
- **Twist** — RGB, TurnUp ring, younger/style-forward buyers
- **Retro** — Classic/understated look, home office, travel

### Social Tier (20–40W RMS)
- **Drift** — 360° sound, silicone handle, living rooms, gyms, casual outdoor

### Party Tier (50W+ RMS)
- **Alpha** — Maximum configurability (bundle your own mic setup, EQ, recording, FM radio)
- **Nova** — Complete out-of-the-box karaoke package

## Common DNA (All Speakers Except Retro)
- Bluetooth 5.0+
- TWS stereo pairing
- Dynamic RGB lighting
- (Retro trades RGB for vintage charm)

## Strategic Summary
- 7 SKUs, each with clear niche — no cannibalization
- Alpha vs Nova: configurability vs completeness
- Drift: mid-range social
- Twist vs Retro: modern RGB vs vintage charm
- BA-20/BA-10: ecosystem accessories pulling buyers deeper into brand
- Visual identity: black dominant, white type, no fixed accent (orange = Alpha hardware only; RGB = product-driven energy)
- Dual origin narrative: "designed in Ibiza" + "100% Filipino-based" = international cred + local trust
- Pricing: all products 30–55% below SRP — reinforces "sulit" positioning
