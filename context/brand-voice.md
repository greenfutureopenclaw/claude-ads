# **Potico.ph — Brand Kit (Final Version)**

## **1. Brand Identity**

**Brand Name:**
Potico.ph

**Tagline:**
*The right gift, every time.*

**Value Proposition:**
Provide an effortless gifting experience through a curated, multi-category selection that fits every budget, backed by reliable same-day delivery and nationwide reach.

**Description:**
Potico.ph is the Philippines' premier destination for curated, high-quality gift boxes and gourmet treats.
You can send something meaningful, without overthinking it—whether it’s last-minute or planned ahead.
From affordable picks to premium bundles, everything is curated for them and delivered on time

---

## **5. Copywriting Guidelines**

### **Core Principles**

* Lead with **emotion (recipient experience)**
* Make **you (the sender)** the hero
* Keep it **short and punchy**
* Balance:

  * **Quality (curated, premium feel)**
  * **Accessibility (fits every budget)**
* Emphasize **convenience + reliability**

---

### **Mandatory Mentions**

* ✅ “Same-day delivery” (Metro Manila priority)

---

### **Writing Style**

* Use **second person (“you”)**
* Short sentences
* No fluff
* Focus on:

  * Thoughtfulness
  * Ease
  * Timing
  * Value

---

## **10. Strategic Reality (Important Insight)**

Potico.ph operates at the intersection of:

* **Convenience (same-day, last-minute)**
* **Affordability (from ~₱199)**
* **Curation (bundles, personalized gifts)** ([potico.tenereteam.com][1])

👉 This means your brand should **never lean too luxury-only**
👉 It wins by being:
**“thoughtful + fast + accessible”**
