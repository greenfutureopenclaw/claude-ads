## Persona 1 — The Panic-Stricken Professional


Tweak / Add: emphasize clear cutoff times, “order by” clock, and guaranteed delivery copy. Add quick-filter products (e.g., “Under 3 hours” / “Delivered today”).

Key Messages / Microcopy

* Headline: “Ordered at 9 AM. Delivered by lunch.”
* Support: “Same-day delivery in Metro Manila — wrapped & ready.”
* CTA: “Send Now — Delivered Today”

Recommended Channels

* Search + branded SEM (“same day gifts”), Social ads (FB/IG) with urgency creatives, SMS remarketing, Checkout CTA.

Product Fit

* Curated single-box gift sets, premium-looking quick-wrap options, “man-approved” collections.

## Cross-Persona Recommendations (actionable)


* Payment UX: Add visible “International cards accepted” badge on checkout for OFWs.
* Delivery trust signals: show live cutoff clock, photo-on-delivery add-on, and substitution policy link.
* Copy rule: Always lead with the *recipient emotion* and end with delivery certainty (“They’ll feel it when it opens. Delivered today.”)
* Trust & proof: Add “orders delivered on time” social proof stat (e.g., “10,000 last-minute gifts delivered”) and a small “photo delivered” gallery.
* Pricing tiers: Show “fits every budget” badges (e.g., ₱499 / ₱999 / ₱1,999) so OFWs and professionals can filter quickly.
* Fulfillment upsells: Photo/video on delivery, gift-note writing, premium wrap.
