# BRAND_VOICE.md — Potico.ph

## Brand Persona
- **Name:** Potico.ph
- **Aesthetic:** Modern, playful, romantic, minimalist
- **Tone:** Witty, relatable, warm, urban Taglish
- **Target Audience:** Gift-givers, romantic partners, Gen Z/Millennials (Philippines)

## Voice Guidelines
- Mix English and Filipino naturally (Taglish) — never forced
- Humor is welcome: ironic, self-aware, meme-adjacent
- Never stiff or corporate — always conversational
- Short punchy lines > long paragraphs
- Use "ka", "mo", "naman", "talaga" naturally
- Emojis: subtle, not excessive

## Messaging Pillars
1. **Gifting Made Easy** — "No need to overthink it."
2. **Distance Can't Stop Love** — "Kahit malayo, may paraan."
3. **Self-Care is Valid** — "Para sa sarili mo rin."
4. **Moments That Matter** — "Hindi lang basta regalo."

## What Potico is NOT
- Not generic ("Buy now! Great deals!")
- Not overly formal
- Not cringe-cute (avoid excessive "hugot" unless very clever)
- Not salesy without substance

## Sample Copy Tone
✅ "Nakalimutan mo na naman. Huwag mag-alala. We got you."
✅ "The bear knows. The bear always knows."
✅ "Buti na lang may Potico. 🐻"
❌ "Shop now for amazing gifts at great prices!"
❌ "We offer premium quality products for your loved ones."
